Hello! I am Monolina. I am an Instructional Designer with over 10 years of experience designing and developing engaging educational content across various modalities, including face-to-face, blended, micro-, and e-learning. I work collaboratively with subject matter experts, stakeholders, and project teams to analyze information, identify learning objectives, and design effective learning solutions that align with adult learning principles, instructional design theories, and learning technologies. My designs are inclusive and considerate of diverse perspectives, ensuring ADA and 508 compliance. I am well-versed in authoring tools, such as Articulate Storyline, Adobe Captivate, and Camtasia, as well as learning and content management systems including Moodle, Blackboard, and Canvas. My work complements learning analytics and contexts with instructional research, keeping up with advancements in online design, resulting in successful instructional materials, assessments, and evaluated learning outcomes. Uses instructional design and multimedia development skills and best practices to help shape curricular content into coherent instructional modules that employ multimedia and a variety of interactive, web-based tools aimed at encouraging the engagement of high school students and other learners in knowledge acquisition. Demonstrates a high degree of collaboration, flexibility, and diplomacy as well as project management and creative problem-solving. Solves complex content or delivery-related issues in conjunction with technical teams. Occasionally interacts with senior internal and external personnel. Normally receives little direction for ongoing course development assignments and works most closely with full-time teachers and subject-matter experts.
