Hello! I am Monolina. As an Instructional Designer, I am responsible for designing and developing effective learning solutions that empower learners to apply their knowledge in new contexts. I specialize in creating engaging face-to-face, blended, micro-, and e-learning in synchronous and asynchronous modalities ensuring that the content is ADA and 508-compliant and represents diverse perspectives. I work collaboratively with subject matter experts, stakeholders, and project teams to gather and analyze information, identify learning objectives, and design effective learning solutions that align with adult learning principles, instructional design theories, learning technologies, QM Rubrics, and Universal Design for Learning. I design and develop instructional materials, create assessments, and evaluate learning outcomes. My work complements learning analytics and contexts with instructional research while keeping up with advancements in online design.
